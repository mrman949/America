Helping you get a Job!

Maintain all professionalism during each stage of getting a job.
Avoid slang and misconduct.
Only tell the truth, it is grevious to be caught in a lie.

Arrive on time to each part of the interviewing process. Employers monitor ethic and habits, and appreciate punctuality

Ask questions during an interview that pertain to what capacity you will work. Never ask the pay (most can't tell you)


Never make an employer feel uneasy speaking to you. You should appear as outstanding as possible.

During an interview maintain your complete undivided attention on the interview. Never accept a phone call during an interview.

Only tell your employer what they need to know about you, things that would affect your ability to work.
