Openings

The beginning is standard: Add name (in print), address, and contact information.

You don't have to be detailed when you tell where you learned about the company (but if you were refeered by a friend, they may get a bonus out of it) This is where you put in your desired position, start date, current employment, and pay range. Write the full position, write the date completly, pay range should be reasonable or as listed, and current employment should be company name.

This part of the application is where you show your qualifications.
Full name, city, and state for every school.
Tell your type of degree or qualification from each institute.
State your major

The skills you've aquired over the institutions above, or skills you've obtained on your own should be listed here.
