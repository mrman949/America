Resume and Coverletter

This is a standard application from for employment
This is your resume simplified.
The beginning is standard add name (in print),address, and contact info.

You don't have to tell where you learned about the company
(but if you were refeered by a friend, they may get a bonus out of it)

This is where you put in your desired position, start date,
current employment, and pay range.

Write the full position, write the date completly, 
pay range should be reasonable or as listed, and current
employment should be company name.
This part of the application is where you show your qualifications.

Full name, city, and state for every school.

Tell your type of degree or qualification from each institute.

State your major
​The skills you've aquired over the institutions above, or skills
you've obtained on your own should be listed here.
